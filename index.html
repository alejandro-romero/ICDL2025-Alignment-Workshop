Workshop: Alignment through Purpose in Autonomous Robots

ICDL 2025 — September 2025, Madrid, Spain

Overview

This half-day workshop aims to explore how defining a clear purpose in autonomous robotic systems can enhance alignment with human expectations, ethical standards, and safety requirements. It will bring together experts from robotics, AI, neuroscience, psychology, ethics, and cognitive science to foster interdisciplinary discussion.

Topics of Interest

  • Engineering approaches to alignment (e.g., RL, human-in-the-loop, constraints)
  • Ethical and societal implications of purpose-driven alignment
  • Developmental and cognitive insights from neuroscience and psychology
  • Cross-disciplinary collaboration frameworks
  • Real-world case studies and applications

Format

  • Invited talks
  • Oral presentations
  • Interactive poster session
